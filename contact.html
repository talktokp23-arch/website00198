Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our chess academy

What age groups do you teach?

We welcome students from age 5 to adults. Our programs are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced tournament players.

Do you offer online coaching?

Yes! We provide high-quality online coaching sessions using modern chess platforms. This allows us to work with students globally while maintaining the same level of instruction quality.

How quickly can I expect to improve?

Improvement varies by individual, but most students see noticeable progress within 2-3 months of regular training. Dedicated students often achieve significant rating improvements within 6-12 months.

What makes your academy special?

Our combination of experienced grandmaster coaches, personalized training approaches, modern technology, and proven track record of student success sets us apart from other chess academies.
